About Omega Epsilon Chapter

The Omega Epsilon Chapter of Alpha Epsilon Pi at James Madison University represents a legacy of brotherhood, scholarship, and leadership. Since our founding, we have been committed to developing leaders for the Jewish community and beyond.

As the world's leading Jewish fraternity, Alpha Epsilon Pi provides our members with opportunities for personal growth, academic excellence, and lifelong friendships. Our chapter has been recognized as JMU's Chapter of the Year, demonstrating our commitment to excellence in all areas.

We are dedicated to our core values: developing leadership for the Jewish community, fostering scholarship, and promoting brotherhood. Through philanthropy, campus involvement, and community service, we strive to make a positive impact at JMU and in Harrisonburg.

Our History

The Omega Epsilon Chapter of Alpha Epsilon Pi was founded at James Madison University in 2006 and chartered in 2008 with a vision to create a home for Jewish students and allies seeking the best possible college and fraternity experience.

Alpha Epsilon Pi was founded in 1913 at New York University, and today represents over 180 chapters worldwide. As part of this international brotherhood, our chapter carries forward the legacy and values that have defined AEPi for over a century.

Throughout our history at JMU, we have achieved remarkable milestones including being named the SOAR Chapter of the Year, maintaining strong academic performance and achieving the highest gpa among fraternities for multiple terms, and contributing countless hours to community service and philanthropic causes.

Our chapter has produced leaders who have gone on to excel in business, medicine, law, education, and public service. We continue to build on this legacy by developing the next generation of leaders and creating lasting bonds of brotherhood.

Today, the Omega Epsilon Chapter remains committed to our founding principles while adapting to serve the needs of modern students. We are proud of our past and excited for our future as we continue to grow and make an impact at JMU and beyond.
